About The Eras Tour

The Eras Tour is a record-breaking global tour by Taylor Swift, celebrating her 10 eras of music. Each performance is a tribute to her iconic albums and musical journey.

The Eras Tour Q&A

Everything you need to know about The Eras Tour, from ticketing details to special moments during the show.

Where can I buy tickets for The Eras Tour?

Tickets are available through official ticketing platforms like Ticketmaster and authorized resellers. Be cautious of third-party sellers to avoid scams.

What time do the doors open for the concert?

Doors typically open 2-3 hours before the concert starts. We recommend arriving early to avoid long queues.

What items are allowed inside the venue?

Small bags, phones, and friendship bracelets are allowed. However, large bags, professional cameras, and recording devices are usually prohibited.

Can I bring friendship bracelets to trade?

Yes! Trading friendship bracelets is a fun tradition among fans. Just be mindful of security checks.

How do surprise songs work?

Each show features two unique surprise songs that are different from previous concerts. Taylor Swift usually performs them acoustically.

Where can I buy official tour merchandise?

Official tour merch is available at the venue and online. Merchandise booths may have long lines, so plan accordingly.
